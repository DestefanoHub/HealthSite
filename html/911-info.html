911 Call

  • Take a Deep Breath
  • Call 911
  • What to tell the 911 operator
    • Give the operator your NAME
    • LOCATION of emergency include the Street name
    • PHONE NUMBER you are calling from
    • The reason you are calling
    • Physical Description of the injured person(s)
    • Describe injuries or symptoms of person needing medical emergency
    • Listen to the operator's instruction
    • Answer all of their questions
    • STAY on the the line until the operator instructs you to hang up
