Allergic Reactions

When To Call 911

  • If the person experiencing the allergic reaction is showing some of the more serious symptoms of a severe allergic reaction such as swelling of the face, eyes, or tongue, call 911 or get the person to an emergency facility.
  • If any of the life threatening allergic reaction symptoms occur PLEASE CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY, Without treatment a person can DIE within 15 minutes.

Symptoms

Signs Of Severe Allergic Reactions

  • Abdominal cramping or pain.
  • Pain or tightness in the chest.
  • Diarrhea.
  • Difficulty swallowing.
  • Dizziness (vertigo).
  • Fear or anxiety.
  • Flushing of the face.
  • Nausea or vomiting.
  • Heart palpitations.
  • Swelling of the face, eyes, or tongue.
  • Weakness.
  • Wheezing.
  • Difficulty breathing.
  • Unconsciousness.

Signs Of Life-Threatening Allergic Reactions

  • Swelling of the airway.
  • Inability to breathe.
  • Sudden and severe drop in blood pressure.

What To Do

Treatment of Allergic Reactions

  • If a person is showing any serious symptoms of a severe allergic reaction or life threatening allergic reaction
    • Check if the person is breathing. If they are not, administer CPR.
    • Ask or check if the person has an EpiPen. If they do, help them administer the Epipen.
  • If the person is unconscious
    • Lay the person flat on their back and elevate their legs.
    • Place a blanket over them. This will prevent the person from going into shock.

How to Administer an EpiPen

  1. Remove EpiPen from the carrie tube.
  2. Grasp the Epipen with the orange tip facing downwards.
  3. Pull off the blue safety release on the top of the EpiPen.
  4. Align orange tip near outer thigh.
  5. Pull arm back and swing at 90 degree angle.
  6. Push the EpiPen into the thigh until you hear the EpiPen click. The EpiPen is suppose to work through clothing.
  7. Hold the EpiPen against the thigh for 10 seconds.
  8. Remove the EpiPen.
  9. Massage injection site for 10 seconds.
  10. Get emergency assistance.
  11. Put EpiPen back in case and take it with you to the hospital.

How To Perform CPR

  1. Give 30 chest compressions.
  2. Give 2 rescue breaths.
  3. Do not stop.
